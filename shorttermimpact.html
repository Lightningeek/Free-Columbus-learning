Short-Term Impact



Spain and Europe now knew that there was more to the world than just them. The world could be more easily charted and they could expand their territory farther than they ever thought they could. Then they could bring medicine and spice to make better food and cure people faster. Natives were killed and slaughtered and wars decimated land across America by Columbus, forever painting his reputation as a bloothirsty murderer. Smallpox was a common killer. The slave trade was opened as Europeans saw a new market for them in North American settlers.
