Background

Columbus was born on 1451, probably in Genoa, Italy. His real name is Cristoforo Columbo, which was Anglicized to Christopher Columbus. He had no schooling. Before the big expedition, he had sailed on small business for his father.

The wealth from Asia was trickling into Europe for more than 200 years, and Europe was eager for more of it. Asian goods were scarce and expensive because they had to come by a dangerous overland route. Ships could carry them more cheaply and in greater quantity.
